Evolution of Our Leo Club

Leo District 306 A2 is one of the leading Leo Districts in Sri Lanka. It is sponsored and operates under the International Association of Lions Club District 306 A2. Currently, 27 Leo clubs are actively serving the community. Few years back a group of undergraduates from the Sri Lanka Technological Campus joined their hands to lay the foundation for the Leo Club of SLTC with the help of Lion Noel Palliyaguru.

Charter Date: 14th of September 2016
Lions Club: Lions Club of Polgasowita

Experience in Leoism

Leoistic Year 2016/17

President - Leo Navod Kiriwaththuduwa
Vice President - Leo Pemal Yasith
Secretary - Leo Raseema Sugandi
Treasurer - Leo Chathurya Jayalath
Advisor - Lion Noel Palliyaguru

Under the presidency of Leo Navod Kiriwaththuduwa, our Leos were able to conduct many projects for the betterment of society. The Leos were awarded as winners in the Leo district Conference for most of the projects they have conducted during their charter year. They were “Samaye Seenu Handa” under the Peace and Religious activities category and under the Public relationship category, “LEO CODCON” under the sports and recreation category, "Kind" under the Helping hand differently-abled category, "Night Watch" under the Joint project with outside organization, "Aspiration" under the Best project for day celebration category. For the individual awards, Leo Navod Kiriwaththuduwa was awarded many titles and they were the “Most popular Leo”, “Best New Leo of the year” and “Most outstanding new club president” and Lion Noel Palliyaguru was awarded as the “Most Outstanding Leo advisor” and our club was awarded as the “Most outstanding new Leo club”.

Leoistic Year 2017/18

President - Leo Pemal Yasith
Vice President - Leo Werosh Thanushka
Secretary - Leo Danushi Wijewardana
Treasurer - Leo Isuri Udithamala
Advisor - Lion Noel Palliyaguru

Under the presidency of Leo Pemal Yasith, our fellow Leos conducted projects under their categories, "Golden nets", "Save the whales", "Say No to Drugs", "Jeewana yathra" and "Felicitation ceremony" were few of them. We were able to achieve many awards in the District Leo conference at the end of the Leoistic year. Our Leo club was named as winners for most of the projects conducted by our fellow Leos. “A2 Sports day” under the sports and recreation category, “El Talento” under the public relationship category, “Pancha prayama” under the betterment of Leoism category, “heart for hunger” under the project for hunger category. For the individual awards, Leo Navod Kiriwaththuduwa was awarded as the “Most outstanding activity director” and he was also awarded as the “Most outstanding immediate past club president”.

Leoistic Year 2018/19

President - Leo Werosh Thanushka
Vice President - Leo Lakshitha Sandaruwan
Secretary - Leo Heshini Alakoon
Treasurer - Leo Prabodya Chandrasekara
Advisor - Lion Noel Palliyaguru

During the Leoistic year 2018/2019, we were able to overcome many obstacles to keep our Leo club steady by conducting projects such as “Beginning of the spirit” by focusing the students of Pitumpe primary school, “Sandahiru abhivandana”, “Ohuta sewanak” where our fellow Leos presented a shelter for a lottery seller, “Maatha”, “Leo office room”, “Winfest career concert”, “Samayan” and “Game-A-Thon”. During the Leo district conference, we were awarded many titles. Leo Lakshitha Sandaruwan was announced as the "Most outstanding club vice president", Leo Heshani Alahakoon was awarded as the "Most Decent Leo" and Leo Pemal Yasith was awarded as the "Most outstanding immediate past club president" and Leo Isuri Udithamala was named as the “Best Unit member”.

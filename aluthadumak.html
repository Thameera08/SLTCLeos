අළුත් ඇදුමක්

The SLTC Leos organized a project to provide clothes to children and elders in need in the community. The project involved planning and fundraising, collecting and sorting clothes, and distributing the clothes to beneficiaries. The club members successfully raised funds and collected clothes from community members, and distributed them to those in need through partnerships with local charities and shelters. Project “Aluth Andumak” play an important role in supporting the infrastructure of the children's & elders. We aim to building social capital, and helps to reduce poverty and inequality. When children's & elders have access to appropriate clothing, they are better able to protect themselves from the elements, maintain hygiene, and reduce the risk of illness and disease as well.

What is LEOISM?

Leo Club is a youth organisation of Lions Club International. The acronym LEO stands for Leadership, Experience, Opportunity.The Leo Club objective is, to provide the youth of the world with an opportunity for development and contribution, individually and collectively, as responsible members of the local, national and international community. Leo Club encourages the youth to develop leadership qualities by participating in social service activities. They are dependent on a Lions club to sponsor and initiate a Leo club. Leo Club members are addressed as "Leos." They conduct various projects in the fields of health care, elders, children, differently abled, literacy and education, and self-development. Leo Club is the largest youth club of voluntary character in the world , present in 140 countries with over 160,000 members.

LEO Advisor's Message

The acronym LEO stands for Leadership, Experience, and Opportunity. If I may connect the dots in one sentence, Leoism offers you an Opportunity (one and then many) to acquire Experience to develop your Leadership skills. The objective of Leoism is unique: Leoism allows you to take your leadership skills to the next level by creating a non-judgmental and safe space for you to make mistakes, learn from them, receive feedback, and grow.

The best way to learn how to lead is to start leading. To learn certain skills, we have to plunge right in; through practice and trials and errors, we can get better. If we do not do it, we cannot receive feedback. To develop your Leadership skills, you have to start leading. This applies to public speaking as well. To be able to speak, you have to start speaking. There is no beating around the bush or shortcuts. Books may give you theoretical knowledge, but the world we live in is “VUCA”— Volatile, Uncertain, Complex, Ambiguous. In the VUCA world, theoretical knowledge may not be of much use. That is why the Leoism Experience is an ideal Opportunity for aspiring professionals.

Another value that is as important as leadership, experience, and opportunity is Fellowship. When you work with Leos from other Leo clubs in your own district, other districts, and beyond Sri Lanka, the quality of fellowship grows. As you start your professional life, you will have to work with people coming from diverse backgrounds. Your team can include people from different cultures, religions, nationalities, and people who are different to you in the language they speak, political view, and in personality. The ability to work with people having diverse values and cultures is extremely important for organizations as their operations scale globally.

Having these qualities can give you’re a competitive advantage, especially when you are looking for a job. You may be well versed in a particular engineering or technical skill, or you may have an unblemished academic record, but employers are not solely looking for these qualities. Competition in the job market is high, and there are many people who have accumulated technical experience and knowhow. But the values that are nurtured in Leoism, such as leadership and fellowship, stand you out from the flock. If you have had the Leo experience, opportunities come knocking at you, or you may find opportunities where others may not see them.

At one point, you may no longer be a Leo. Therefore, I urge you to make use of this limited-time Opportunity and join Leoism, which is a rare yet great opportunity for youth. Apply values you nurture in Leoism outside Leoism. These values can take you a long way, both in your personal life and in your professional life.

I wish you all the best in life.

Lion wasantha Ramanayeke
Leo Advisor - Leo Club of Sri Lanka Technological Campus
President - Lions Club of Polgasowita

Club President’s Message

My goal is to serve the society and take out them in this current situtation. They will learn how to drag others with them to the ultimate goal of success unselfishly.I feel really honoured to serve as the Club President for the one of the top university Leo Clubs in Sri Lanka. Our Club has been able to produce great leaders with determined personalities. They are the reason behind the Club's success and the current standard.
